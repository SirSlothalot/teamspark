Put it together

Teamspark is a platform that creates teams and matches them with ideas. By analysing all the ideas people have posted and analysing the profile of each user, Teamspark's algorithms will form teams and assign those teams to ideas.

Step 1: Analysing an idea

Teamspark considers the description of an idea, the skills required to complete that idea, the location of its creator and the time the creator is looking to commit.

Step 2: Find who matches an idea

Teamspark identifies people best suited to an idea by comparing an idea's details with user profiles.

Step 3: Form teams from the matched people

Teamspark forms teams by finding people in a similar location, with similar interests, of similar age and who, as a team, have the skills required to work on an idea.
